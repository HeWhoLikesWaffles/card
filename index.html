Chicken that has been baked in a dish

  • Time: somewhat less than a fortnight
  • Serves: 1
Ingredient Quantity
Baking dish 1
Chicken 1
Oven 1

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit.

Step 2: Remove the bag containing the chicken's endocrine system from the chicken.

Step 2: Place the chicken (sans endocrine system) into the clean dish.

Step 3: Place the chicken inside of the preheated oven without burning yourself.

Step 4: Remove the cooked chicken from the oven without burning yourself.

Step 5: Verify the chicken's doneness by probing it with an instrument.

Step 6: Consume.

Boiled water

  • Time: less than the time it takes for light to travel one lightyear in a vacuum
  • Serves: 1
Ingredient Quantity
Cooking pot 1
Water 1
Stovetop 1

Step 1: Turn on one of the stovetop's burners.

Step 2: Fill the cooking pot with clean water, e.g. from a faucet.

Step 3: Place the water-filled pot on the burner and leave it there until it is boiled.

Step 3: Consume.
